Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Welcome to Euro International Overseas Consultancy. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

To provide visa and job placement services, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Details: Name, Email Address, Phone Number, Date of Birth.
  • Professional Details: Resume/CV, Education Certificates, Work Experience.
  • Travel Documents: Passport copies, Visa history (only if required for processing).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely for the purpose of facilitating your overseas career goals:

  • To evaluate your profile for eligibility in European countries.
  • To submit visa and work permit applications on your behalf.
  • To contact you regarding job updates or interview schedules.

3. Data Protection

We take security seriously. Your documents are stored specifically for application processing. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We only share your information with trusted third parties necessary for your process, such as:

  • Embassies and Consulates (for Visa filing).
  • Employer Partners (for Job interviews).
