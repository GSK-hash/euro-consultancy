Frequently Asked Questions

Which countries do you process visas for?

How much time does the process take?

It varies by country. Usually, Poland takes 3-4 months, while Gulf countries take 15-20 days.

We assist with interviews and placements with verified employers. Success depends on your profile and interview performance.
